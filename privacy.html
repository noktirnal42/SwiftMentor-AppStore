Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Overview

SwiftMentor is a Mac learning app for Apple development. The app is designed to keep learning progress, settings, and local content on your device unless a feature clearly requires an external service that you choose to use.

Data We Do Not Collect

SwiftMentor does not require an app account, does not operate a user-tracking advertising system, and does not collect payment card information. App Store purchases and TestFlight distribution are handled by Apple.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store learning progress, completion status, local settings, snippets, and installed add-on pack state on your Mac so the app can restore your learning workspace.

Optional Services

If a future SwiftMentor feature lets you configure an external service, such as an AI provider or content service, information you send to that service is governed by that service's own privacy practices. SwiftMentor should only use those services when you configure or choose them.

TestFlight

If you install SwiftMentor through TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs, diagnostics, and beta feedback according to Apple's TestFlight and App Store privacy practices.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the SwiftMentor support page.
