Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

DevelopMentor stores learning progress, quiz state, settings, and installed add-on pack information locally on your Mac. The app does not require a user account for the base learning experience.

Data We Do Not Collect

DevelopMentor does not operate a user-tracking advertising system and does not collect payment card information. Purchases are handled through App Store purchase systems.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store learning progress, completion status, local settings, snippets, and installed add-on pack state on your Mac so the app can restore your learning workspace.

Optional Services

If a future feature lets you configure an external service, such as an AI provider or content service, information you send to that service is governed by that service's own privacy practices. DevelopMentor should only use those services when you configure or choose them.

TestFlight

If you install DevelopMentor through TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs, diagnostics, and beta feedback according to Apple's TestFlight and App Store privacy practices.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the DevelopMentor support page.
